Brand Identity · 2024

LUME Coffee & Bakery

Description
Concept
Process
Visual identity, packaging and café touchpoints for LUME, a neighbourhood coffee and bakery brand. The system extends across cups, bags, receipts and campaign imagery.
The concept focuses on soft light and everyday rituals. A deep green palette, warm typography and simple compositions echo the calm experience of a slow morning at LUME.
From early sketches to final mockups, the project explored logo proportions, packaging formats and photography angles. Each element was tested in realistic café settings to ensure cohesion.
