About Aida

Aida is a graphic designer focused on brand identities, print and digital experiences. Her work balances clean typography with warm, tactile details to make brands feel human and memorable.

From early concepts to final production files, she guides clients through a clear process—listening carefully, exploring visually, and refining until every detail feels right.
